Wedding Album Design

Master professional photo editing techniques for weddings, portraits, and commercial use. This course focuses on both creative and technical aspects of image enhancement, preparing you for work in photography studios, events, and digital media.

  • ● Photo enhancement and restoration – RAW image processing, tonal adjustments, and color correction
  • ● Portrait and product retouching – Skin smoothing, frequency separation, dodging & burning, and blemish removal
  • ● Compositing and creative effects – Layering, masking, background replacement, and artistic effects

Design elegant, print-ready wedding albums and build a polished portfolio showcasing your retouching and design skills.

  • ● 2D & 3D Motion Design – Animated text, logo reveals, infographics, and visual storytelling

Printing Media Design

  • ● Prepress & Production – CMYK color mode, bleed settings, DPI resolution, and file formats
